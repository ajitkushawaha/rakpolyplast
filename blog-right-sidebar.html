About Us

Established in the year 2023 at Noida, India, we Ram Ashray Kushwaha are a Proprietorship-based firm, engaged as the foremost manufacturer and supplier of PP granules, among other products.

Ram Ashray Kushwaha is the parent company that provides manpower services, while RAK Polyplast operates as its subsidiary, focusing on manufacturing high-quality PP granules.

Welcome to Our Factory

Take a virtual tour of our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility where we produce high-quality PP granules. Our factory is equipped with the latest technology and advanced automation systems to ensure top-notch quality and efficiency.

We follow strict safety and environmental protocols to maintain a sustainable and eco-friendly production process. Our dedicated team of engineers and technicians ensures that every stage of manufacturing adheres to industry standards.

With a focus on continuous innovation, we incorporate cutting-edge techniques to enhance product durability and performance. Our research and development department constantly works on improving formulations to meet customer requirements.
